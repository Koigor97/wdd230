Site Purpose

Lynnwood Business Chamber website would serve as a one stop online resource for all local businesses and community members within the Lynnwood Tri-state area. Our mission is to foster economic development, prioritizing the growth of the local businessman and woman, and pushing for lucrative business policies and ideas in our community. As a chamber of commerce organization, we provide a wide range of services which includes, networking events, fundraising in support of small businesses, creating an environment for educational opportunities and many more. By bringing local businesses together, connecting with each other, and the community being at the heart of it, we can achieve a thriving local economy. Together as one family unit, we can build a stronger and more prosperous community for all.
